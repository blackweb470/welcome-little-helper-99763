## 3. CORE FEATURES

### A. Proactive Chat Rules
Automatically engage visitors on your website based on specific behavioral triggers:
- **Time on Page:** Triggers after a visitor spends X seconds on the page.
- **Specific Page:** Triggers when a visitor lands on a particular URL (e.g., pricing page).
- **Exit Intent:** Triggers when a visitor moves their mouse to leave the page, reducing bounce rates.
- **Scroll Depth:** Triggers after a visitor scrolls X% down the page.

### B. Ticketing System
Automatically creates support tickets from chat conversations to help track unresolved issues.
- **When created:** When a live agent conversation ends unresolved, is manually escalated, or a visitor requests human support.
- **Properties:** Status (open, in_progress, resolved, closed), Priority (low, medium, high, urgent), and Assignable to specific team members.

### C. Voice Chat (BETA)
Allows visitors to speak naturally with the AI assistant using their microphone.
- **Capabilities:** Real-time voice recognition, natural language understanding, voice responses from AI, automatic transcription, and sentiment analysis.
- **Tech Stack:** Powered by OpenAI Realtime API via Supabase Edge Functions.

### D. Live Agent Transfer
Available on Pro and Business plans, allowing a seamless transition from the AI bot to a human agent, including the entire conversation history.

## 4. WHATSAPP BUSINESS INTEGRATION

Lyqn AI integrates natively with the Meta WhatsApp Business API.
- **Setup Requirements:** A verified Meta Business Account and an App created in the Meta Developer Portal.
- **Whitelisting:** Your domain must be whitelisted (e.g., lyqn.app) in the Meta App settings.
- **Manual Setup:** If businesses face domain whitelisting errors from Meta, Lyqn provides a Manual Setup route using a Phone Number ID, WhatsApp Business Account ID, and a Permanent Access Token.
- **Functionality:** Both the AI and your live agents can respond to WhatsApp messages from the unified Lyqn dashboard.

## 5. INSTALLATION & SETUP

**Web Widget Installation:**
1. Include the provided JavaScript embed code in the `<head>` or `<body>` tag of your website.
2. The code snippet initializes the widget on every page where it is placed.
3. To test the widget before going live, you can use the `/widget-demo` route in the Lyqn dashboard.

**Signup & Onboarding Flow:**
1. Businesses sign up using email/password.
2. If it's a new account, they are redirected to the pricing page.
3. They select a plan (e.g., Basic for a 1-month free trial) and add their payment card via the Polar checkout.
4. They immediately get access to the dashboard. The card is only charged after the 1-month trial ends.
5. Subscriptions can be managed, upgraded, downgraded, or canceled directly from the `/billing` page in the dashboard.

## 6. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS FROM BUSINESSES (Bot Q&A)

**Q: Do you offer a free trial?**
A: Yes! Our Basic Plan ($9.99/month) comes with a 1-month free trial. You can test the platform completely risk-free before being charged.

**Q: Can customers talk to the AI using their voice?**
A: Yes, if you are on the Pro or Business plan, we offer a Voice Chat feature (currently in BETA) that allows real-time voice recognition and AI voice responses using professional-grade audio processing.

**Q: What happens if the AI cannot solve a customer's issue?**
A: If you are on the Pro or Business plan, the AI can seamlessly transfer the conversation to a live human agent. Furthermore, our Ticketing System will automatically create a ticket for unresolved issues so your team can track and follow up later.

**Q: How do I install the chat widget on my website?**
A: It's very simple. You just copy a short HTML `<script>` snippet from your dashboard and paste it into your website's code. It works on almost any website platform.

**Q: Does the platform integrate with WhatsApp?**
A: Absolutely. We have a complete Meta WhatsApp integration. You can connect your WhatsApp Business account via the Meta Developer Portal and manage all WhatsApp conversations directly inside the Lyqn dashboard.

**Q: What if I have multiple businesses?**
A: The Basic plan supports up to 3 businesses. If you have more, the Pro plan supports up to 10, and the Business plan ($99.99/month) supports unlimited businesses.

**Q: How do proactive chat rules work?**
A: You can set up smart triggers in the dashboard to automatically open the chat and send a message. For example, you can trigger a message when a user is about to leave the site (Exit Intent), when they scroll halfway down the page (Scroll Depth), or after they spend 30 seconds on your pricing page.

**Q: How can I cancel my subscription?**
A: You can manage your subscription, view your next billing date, change plans, or cancel directly from the `/billing` page in your dashboard.

**Q: Does the AI learn from my business documents?**
A: Yes, if you are on the Business plan ($99.99/month), you have access to AI Learning and Business Documents support, allowing the AI to give highly specific answers based on your uploaded materials.
